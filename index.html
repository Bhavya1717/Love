  • Unveiling Unspoken Affections

    Nidhi, a kind and perfect soul, continued to communicate with me, albeit strictly in a platonic sense. Our bond evolved into a close friendship, but it was during this time that I noticed a mutual social media acquaintance showing interest in her.

They had shared moments of daily devotion, visiting temples, and even exchanging kisses. Their separation had been a result of familial disapproval, particularly from the guy's mother.
